# Click&Boat

**Summary**: European P2P and professional boat rental marketplace. Strong Mediterranean presence. Charges both owners and renters. Closest structural competitor to YachtBay.

---

## Overview

| | |
|---|---|
| **Model** | P2P + professional owners |
| **Scale** | Strong Europe/Mediterranean presence |
| **Headquarters** | France |
| **Booking model** | Instant book available |
| **Charter types** | Day trips, multi-day, weekly |
| **Vessel types** | Sailboats, motorboats, catamarans, inflatable boats, barges, jet skis |

## Commission Structure

Charges **both sides**:
- Owner: up to **23%** commission (varies by contractual agreement — fleet owners likely get lower rates)
- Renter: service fee added on top (exact % not publicly disclosed, total platform take ~25%)
- Commission and service fees are **non-refundable** on cancellation

## Cancellation Policy

**Owner-defined** — four policy types the owner chooses per listing:

| Policy | Refund | Window |
|---|---|---|
| **Flexible** | 100% | Up to 1 day before |
| **Moderate** | 70% | Up to 10 days before; 0% under 10 days |
| **Strict** | 60% | Up to 30 days before; 0% under 30 days |
| **Custom** | Professional owner sets own terms | — |

Platform fees (commission + service fee) are never refunded regardless of policy.

## Key Features

- Instant book available
- Both private and professional owners (charter companies can list)
- Skipper option per listing
- Multi-language, multi-currency (strong Europe focus)
- Affiliation program
- Mobile app

## Strengths

- Instant book — matches what renters expect
- Strong Mediterranean/European brand and inventory
- Owner-defined cancellation policies give owners flexibility
- Supports both P2P and professional charter companies

## Weaknesses

- High owner commission (up to 23%) — expensive for owners
- Both sides pay — reduces price competitiveness
- Not Greece-specific — generic European platform
- 23% + renter service fee makes total platform take high

## Relevance to YachtBay

- Click&Boat's 23% owner commission vs YachtBay's 13% — we are significantly cheaper for owners
- Their owner-defined cancellation policies may be something to consider for Phase 2 (our MVP uses platform-defined)
- Their instant book is table stakes — we already planned this
- Their "flexible / moderate / strict" cancellation framework is worth adopting as the basis for our Phase 2 per-owner policy options
