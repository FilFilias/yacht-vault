# GetMyBoat

**Summary**: The largest P2P boat and yacht rental marketplace globally. Direct competitor — operates in Greece with 1,100+ listings.

---

## Overview

| | |
|---|---|
| **Model** | P2P marketplace |
| **Scale** | Global, 500,000+ renters, 1,100+ boats in Greece |
| **Headquarters** | USA |
| **Booking model** | Inquiry-based — renter sends inquiry, owner responds with offer |
| **Charter types** | Hourly, day trips, multi-day, weekly |
| **Vessel types** | Sailboats, yachts, powerboats, catamarans, tours, fishing, jet skis, houseboats |

## Commission Structure

Charges **both sides**:
- Owner: ~8–10% (some sources cite 8.5% flat)
- Renter: ~15–20% service fee on top of listing price
- Total platform take: ~23–30% per transaction

Platform service fee is **non-refundable** on cancellation.

## Cancellation Policy

Owner-defined — each listing has its own policy. GetMyBoat default:
- Full refund if cancelled 5+ days before departure
- 24-hour grace period: full refund if cancelled within 24h of booking (regardless of owner policy)
- Owner cancellation: full refund including service fee to renter

## Key Features

- Map-based search with location, date, group size, price, charter type filters
- Featured/promoted listings (owners pay for premium placement)
- Add-on upsells: catering, captain, music packages, insurance
- AI-based demand prediction to recommend optimal pricing to owners
- Review and ratings system (prominent, 5-star scale)
- Mobile app (iOS + Android)

## Strengths

- Largest inventory globally — network effect is strong
- Brand recognition
- AI pricing recommendations for owners
- Add-on ecosystem creates additional revenue

## Weaknesses

- **Inquiry-based booking — NOT instant book.** Renters must wait for owner response. Major friction point.
- High combined fees (~25–30%) make it expensive for both sides
- Generic platform — not yacht/sailing specialist, includes jet skis, kayaks, etc.
- Limited focus on Greek market specifically

## Relevance to YachtBay

- Instant book is a direct differentiator vs GetMyBoat's inquiry model
- Our 13% owner-only commission is significantly cheaper than their combined ~23–30%
- Their Greece presence (1,100+ boats) shows strong supply — we need to compete on owner experience and lower commission
