# AnchorsBook

**Summary**: Boat and yacht rental platform with presence in Greece. Fixed 20% owner-side commission. Interesting local competitor worth monitoring.

---

## Overview

| | |
|---|---|
| **Model** | Marketplace |
| **Scale** | Smaller platform, active in Greece |
| **Booking model** | Online booking |
| **Charter types** | Hourly, full day, weekly, overnight |
| **Vessel types** | Boats, yachts, jet skis |

## Commission Structure

- **Owner-side only**: Fixed **20% commission** on successful bookings
- Renter pays no additional service fee (included in listed price)
- Payout to owner: 12 hours after rental ends via PayPal or Bank Wire

## Key Features

- Identity checks and fraud protection
- Commercial watercraft insurance
- 24/7 customer service
- SMS notifications
- Dedicated vendor account management
- Paid marketing for listed boats
- Includes captain, fuel, taxes in stated price on some listings

## Weaknesses

- Smaller platform — less inventory and brand recognition
- PayPal/Bank Wire payout (slower and more manual than Stripe)
- 20% commission is higher than YachtBay's planned 13%

## Relevance to YachtBay

- **Direct Greek market competitor** — worth monitoring closely
- Their 20% commission vs our 13% — we are cheaper for owners
- Owner-side only commission aligns with our model — validates this approach for the Greek market
- 12-hour payout window is slower than our planned 24–48h post-charter release — we are comparable
- Their insurance inclusion is worth watching — this may be a feature renters expect
