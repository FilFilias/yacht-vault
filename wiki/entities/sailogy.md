# Sailogy

**Summary**: Large yacht charter marketplace, part of Five Seasons Yachting Group. Acquired Borrow A Boat (which had acquired Zizoo). 25,000+ yachts in 1,000+ destinations. Revenue: $17.3M (2026).

---

| **Focus** | Mediterranean, Caribbean, global |

## Notable Events

- **Zizoo** filed for bankruptcy in February 2024 after failing to pass customer payments to charter companies. Subsequently acquired by Borrow A Boat, which was then integrated into Sailogy.
- This consolidation makes Sailogy a larger player but primarily in the professional charter segment — less direct competition for the P2P segment YachtBay targets.

## Relevance to YachtBay

- Primarily serves the professional charter company segment — different supply side than YachtBay's P2P individual owners
- Zizoo's failure highlights the risk of poor payment infrastructure — reinforces the importance of Stripe Connect's automated, transparent payout system
- At 25,000 yachts globally, Sailogy is large but not Greece-focused P2P
- Market consolidation (Sailogy absorbing Zizoo + Borrow A Boat) shows the space is maturing
