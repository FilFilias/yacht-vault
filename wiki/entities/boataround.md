# Boataround

**Summary**: European yacht charter marketplace with a strong presence in Greece. Commission-based model. Known for flexible booking days (not Saturday-Saturday only) and multi-currency support.

---

| | |
|---|---|

| **Charter types** | Day trips, multi-day, flexible days |
| **Vessel types** | Sailboats, catamarans, motorboats |

## Commission Structure

Commission-based (exact % not publicly disclosed). Claims "free cancellation with no extra fees for all bookings" and "booking is always free" for renters — suggests owner-side commission model.

## Notable Features

- **Flexible booking days** — not locked to Saturday–Saturday like traditional charter platforms. Key differentiator.
- Multi-currency support (10+ currencies including EUR, USD, GBP)
- "Up to 45% discount" on advance bookings
- First-minute booking options
- 24/7 customer service
- Verified boats with real pictures

## Weaknesses

- Reliability concerns reported in 2025 — "nightmare cruises" complaints surfaced, payment issues to charter operators
- Less brand recognition than GetMyBoat or Click&Boat
- No clear instant book mention

## Relevance to YachtBay

- Flexible booking days (not just Saturday-Saturday) is something we already planned — confirms this is valued by renters
- Payment reliability issues at Boataround highlight the importance of our Stripe Connect Accounts v2 architecture — automated, reliable payouts are a real differentiator
- Their multi-currency support is something we should have in place before Mediterranean expansion (Phase 3)
