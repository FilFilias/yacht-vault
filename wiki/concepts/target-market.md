# Target Market

**Summary**: Where YachtBay is launching, who it serves, and why Greece is the right starting point.

---

## Launch Market: Greece

Greece is the first and only market at launch.

**Why Greece:**
- One of the world's top sailing and yacht charter destinations
- The founder is a local — gives a structural advantage in recruiting owners (supply side) and building trust
- Strong international tourist demand, especially in summer
- A defined, manageable geography to validate the model before expanding

**Key sailing regions in Greece:**
- Aegean islands (Cyclades, Dodecanese)
- Ionian islands
- Saronic Gulf (closest to Athens, good for day trips)

## Expansion Path

Greece → broader Mediterranean → global. No firm timeline yet; expansion depends on validating the model domestically first.

## Target Customer (Renter)

**Primary**: Mid-range travelers — people who want a quality yacht experience without superyacht pricing. Typically:
- International tourists visiting Greece
- Local Greeks seeking leisure trips
- Small groups (friends, couples, families)

**Secondary** (as inventory grows): Budget day-trippers, sailing enthusiasts (bareboat), corporate/event groups, luxury clients.

Target customers will naturally expand or shift depending on the types of yachts onboarded on the platform.

See [[user-personas]].

## Target Supplier (Owner)

- Individual yacht owners with 1–3 boats
- Small fleet operators (2–10 yachts) — strategic targets for volume discounts
- Professional charter companies looking for an additional booking channel
