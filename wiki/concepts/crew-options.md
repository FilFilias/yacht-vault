# Crew Options

**Summary**: The three crew configurations supported on YachtBay, defined per yacht by the owner.

---

## Overview

Each yacht listing defines which crew configurations are available. This is set by the owner based on the boat type, size, and their own capabilities or staff.

## The Three Configurations

### Bareboat

The renter operates the yacht themselves. No crew provided.

- Typically requires proof of a sailing licence (e.g. RYA, IYT, or equivalent)
- Suitable for experienced sailors
- Usually the most affordable option
- Owner may require a security deposit

### Skippered

A professional skipper is included with the booking. The renter is a passenger/guest — they do not need sailing experience.

- Skipper is provided by the owner
- Most popular configuration for mid-range renters
- Pricing includes the skipper's fee

### Fully Crewed

A skipper plus additional crew (e.g. cook, deckhand, steward) are included.

- Typically for larger yachts (sailing yachts 50ft+, motor yachts, catamarans)
- Premium experience — closest to a luxury charter
- Pricing reflects full crew costs

## Design Notes

- The available configurations are selected by the **owner** at listing time
- A single yacht can offer multiple configurations at different price points
- The platform must clearly display which configurations are available for each yacht
- Bareboat listings may need a licence verification flow — this is a future spec item
