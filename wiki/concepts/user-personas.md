# User Personas

**Summary**: The two primary users of the YachtBay platform — the yacht owner and the renter.

---

## Persona 1: The Owner

**Who they are**: A Greek yacht owner — either an individual with 1–3 boats or a small fleet operator.

**Goals:**
- Maximise occupancy and revenue from their yacht(s)
- Minimise admin — easy calendar management, automated booking confirmations
- Reach renters they wouldn't find through word-of-mouth or local brokers

**Frustrations:**
- Current options are fragmented (local brokers, GetMyBoat, word-of-mouth)
- High broker commissions with little transparency
- Managing bookings manually (WhatsApp, email, phone)

**Key needs from YachtBay:**
- Easy listing creation with photos, specs, pricing, and availability calendar
- Clear commission structure with no surprises
- Reliable payments
- Visibility into booking history and earnings

**Volume owners (fleet operators)** are strategic — they bring multiple listings and get preferential commission rates. See [[commission-model]].

---

## Persona 2: The Renter

**Who they are (primary)**: A mid-range traveler — international tourist or local Greek — looking for a quality yacht experience. Traveling in a small group (couple, friends, family).

**Goals:**
- Find and book a yacht easily without going through a broker
- Compare options transparently (price, features, photos, reviews)
- Feel confident in the booking (secure payment, clear cancellation policy)

**Frustrations:**
- Hard to find yachts online — scattered across broker sites, Facebook groups, direct owner contacts
- Pricing is opaque — hard to compare like-for-like
- No standardised reviews or trust signals

**Key needs from YachtBay:**
- Clean search and comparison experience
- Transparent pricing (no hidden fees)
- Instant booking — no waiting for owner to respond
- Reviews and trust signals
- Clear information on crew options and what's included

**Charter duration**: Both day trips and multi-day/weekly charters. See [[booking-model]].
