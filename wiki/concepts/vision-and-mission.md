# Vision & Mission

**Summary**: The core purpose of YachtBay — what problem it solves and why it exists.

---

## The Problem

Renting a yacht in Greece is fragmented and opaque. Owners advertise on scattered platforms, through brokers, or word-of-mouth. Renters have no single place to browse real inventory, compare prices transparently, and book with confidence.

## The Solution

YachtBay is a peer-to-peer marketplace where yacht owners list their vessels and renters can browse, compare, and instantly book — without middlemen, phone calls, or back-and-forth emails.

## Vision

To become the go-to platform for yacht rentals in Greece, and eventually the Mediterranean — making yacht experiences accessible, transparent, and bookable in minutes.

## Mission

Connect yacht owners with renters through a trusted, easy-to-use platform that handles discovery, comparison, and booking end-to-end.

## Why Greece First

- One of the world's top sailing destinations
- Founder is a local — gives a real advantage in recruiting owners and building trust on the supply side
- Strong existing demand from international tourists and locals alike
- Manageable market size to validate the model before expanding
