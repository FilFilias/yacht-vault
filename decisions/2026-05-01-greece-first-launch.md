# Launch in Greece First

## Context

YachtBay could target multiple markets from day one (e.g. broader Mediterranean) or focus on a single geography to validate the model first.

## Options Considered

- **Greece only**: Focused launch in one market
- **Mediterranean**: Launch across Greece, Croatia, Turkey, Italy simultaneously
- **Global**: Open platform from day one, let supply come from anywhere

## Decision

**Greece only** at launch.

## Consequences

- Founder's local knowledge and network are a real competitive advantage on the supply side — recruiting Greek owners is easier than recruiting internationally
- Greece is one of the top global sailing destinations, so demand is not a concern
- A smaller, defined market makes it easier to iterate, gather feedback, and validate the model
- Expansion to broader Mediterranean is the natural next step once Greece is proven
- Platform must be built with multi-market expansion in mind (multi-currency, multi-language architecture) even if only Greek market is active at launch
